What is BIV?

BEST Inside view is a pioneer initiative organised by BEST Lisboa , partnering with Instituto Superior Técnico . It acts as a job shadowing event , in which the university student follows a professional on his line of interest during a work day. This event counts with 11 editions, being one of the main career support events done in the Lisbon University. BIV is an excellent occasion for the company to show their works and opportunities for the students.

It will includea brief presentation of the companys, approximately 5 minutes, about their áareas of work in Portugal and internationally. the companiess can be present to present their work or they can send us the presentation for us to do it.

Following this, there will be aroundtables sessions that will consist of a conversation with a group of students of around 15 minutes, where the company representative can expose inmore detail their projects.

Finally, there will be a coffee break, providing a networking opportunity for students to clarify any remaining questions they may have.

INSIDE DAYS INSIDE DAYS

The Inside Days will take place in xx and xx during a week previously agreed upon with BEST. Students apply for an Inside Day during that week. However, the specific day and conditions of each visit must be individually arranged between the student and the company.

Inside Days should preferably be individual. In cases where groups are formed, they should not exceed three people. The activities each company conducts are at their discretion, as long as they align with the objectives of the event.

Applying Phase

The first phase of applications for the Inside Days will take place on the day of the Opening Ceremony, with any remaining spots moving to a second phase of applications. The matching between students and companies is entirely done by us, with the aim of satisfying the preferences indicated by the companies as much as possible.

STUDENTS

The students participating in BIV are interested and proactive in discovering the opportunities the job market has to offer, as well as in expanding their professional network. The Opening Ceremony is an opportunity to initially meet the students and establish first contact with them. All participants' résumés will be provided.

PROMOTION

Make your company known on campus. All packages include several posts on social media and the presence of your company's logo on the posters that we will place throughout the entire Técnico.
