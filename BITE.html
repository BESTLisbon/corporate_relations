What is BITE?

The BEST Innovation and Technology Event is a technology competition that encourages students to showcase their skills in strategy, critical thinking, and teamwork. With a successful first edition in March 2024, it is the first event in the field of information technology organized by BEST Lisbon and is open to all students of the University of Lisbon.

The BEST Innovation and Technology Event is a technology competition that encourages students to showcase their skills in strategy, critical thinking, and teamwork. With a successful first edition in March 2024, it is the first event in the field of information technology organized by BEST Lisbon and is open to all students of the University of Lisbon.

OPENING CEREMONY

At the opening ceremony, which takes place at the Instituto Superior Técnico, there will be space for a networking session that provides an opportunity to establish strategic connections between students and companies.

CATHEGORIES

With two categories, participants of BITE will be able to choose between the IT challenge or Case Study, proposed by a partner company (main sponsor) or by the event organizing team.

In the week before the competition, preparation sessions and workshops will be developed for the teams.

These workshops may be conducted by partner companies, professors, or alumni, and the topics can vary from programming languages, machine learning, user experience to project management, how to pitch, among others.

IT CHALLENGE

Participants can compete in the IT challenge category where they will discover the theme proposed by BEST Lisbon or by the main sponsor company of the category during the opening ceremony. During the 24-hour competition, each team will develop a technological solution in software as an integral part of their project related to the proposed theme.

This category has access to the mentoring program, where each team is guided by a mentor who helps them plan and structure their project before the competition.

CASE STUDY

The Case Study category involves finding a theoretical solution to a current problem in the field of engineering by developing a potential strategy to resolve the addressed issue within 24 hours and with limited resources, through intensive study and analysis of existing technology.

This year, we decided to divide the Case Study category into three parts of 8 hours each, allowing for a strategic approach to each part of the Case Study.

This format, with breaks, promotes concentration, creativity, and interactions among the participating teams, ensuring harmony between the two categories of BITE.

DURING THE 24 HOURS...

Several mini-competitions lasting 20 to 30 minutes will be included with the aim of stimulating and increasing the participants' energy levels in a more relaxed yet equally team-focused environment. These simple yet intriguing small challenges can also be proposed by partner companies.

APPLYING PHASE

The first phase of applications for the Inside Days will take place on the day of the Opening Ceremony, with any remaining spots moving to a second phase of applications. The matching between students and companies is entirely done by us, with the aim of satisfying the preferences indicated by the companies as much as possible.

WHY PARTNERING WITH BEST?

MEET THE STUDENTS

The students participating in BITE are interested and proactive in discovering the opportunities the job market has to offer, as well as in expanding their professional network. The Opening Ceremony is an opportunity to initially meet the students and establish first contact with them. All participants' résumés will be provided.

PROMOTION

Make your company known on campus. All packages include several posts on social media and the presence of your company's logo on the posters that we will place throughout the entire Técnico.
