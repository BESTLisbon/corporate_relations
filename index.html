ABOUT US

As one of the 85 BEST Local groups that exist all over Europe, we are a non governmental, apolitical organization that has the mission of Empowering Diversity and Developing Students.

BEST organises a set of events that mainly focus on providing unique experiences to technology students and on complementing their education in the perspective that these experiences would help them in their future career path.

Furthermore, BEST events also have a very strong international component that cultivates a spirit of integration and diversity.

OUR EVENTS OUR EVENTS
BITE
Best Innovation and Technology Experience

Best Innovation and Technology Experience is an engineering competition organized by BEST Lisbon and aimed at college students. It challenges participant teams to compete for 24 hours in one of 2 categories, an IT Challenge or a Case Study.

Each team can choose between putting their coding skills to test or coming up with new solutions to a real life problem. During the week leading up to the event, there will be many workshops where competitors can develop their hard and soft skills as preparation for the event.

BIV
BEST Inside View

BEST Inside view is a pioneer initiative organised by BEST Lisboa , partnering with Instituto Superior Técnico . It acts as a job shadowing event , in which the university student follows a professional on his line of interest during a work day. This event counts with 11 editions, being one of the main career support events done in the Lisbon University. BIV is an excellent occasion for the company to show their works and opportunities for the students.

This project gives students the opportunity to have their first contact with the business world. This initiative provides participants with a clearer view of their field of study and the various possibilities for their future careers.

BEE
BEST Entrepreneurship Experience

The BEST Entrepreneurship Experience is an event that brings together students from all over the University of Lisbon who want to develop their entrepreneurial skills by working on a business idea that addresses a current challenge.

This event takes place at the beginning of May and currently has two successful editions in 2023 and 2024.

BEST Courses
BEST Seasonal Courses

BEST courses take place in the four seasons of the year - summer, autumn, winter, and spring. They are events for technology students from other European cities where BEST is present, offering students the opportunity to: complement their field of study; visit companies, research centers, etc.; participate in case studies; attend classes on various engineering and technology topics; and earn ECTS credits in recognized courses.
