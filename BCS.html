What is BC?

BEST courses take place in the four seasons of the year - summer, autumn, winter, and spring. They are events for technology students from other European cities where BEST is present, offering students the opportunity to: complement their field of study; visit companies, research centers, etc.; participate in case studies; attend classes on various engineering and technology topics; and earn ECTS credits in recognized courses.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

During these ~10-day events, students participate in classes given by professors or industry experts, and at the end of the course, performance is assessed through an exam.

The subtypes of courses we offer are: Technology courses, Career-related skills courses, and Applied engineering courses.

WORKSHOPS

As our partner, the company has the opportunity to conduct a workshop on a topic related to one of its working methods or a subject that could be useful for the participants to improve their academic performance related to the course theme or to enhance their performance and development of the project they will undertake during the week.

This activity is a good combination with a company visit but can also be carried out independently. The duration of the activity is flexible, depending on the theme and content of the workshop. This content needs to be aligned with the general academic objectives of the course or be relevant to its performance. For this purpose, BEST Lisbon has an Academic Coordinator for the course to help and guide the process of preparing this activity.

The hours spent with the company count towards the total number of hours that students are required to complete.

Throughout the course hours, companies play a crucial role in enhancing the experience.

They can provide theoretical and practical classes, workshops, group seminars, guide laboratory work, fieldwork, and company visits.

The hours spent with the company count towards the total number of hours that students are required to complete.

NETWORKING

The networking session is an opportunity to have closer contact with both the participants and the local IST students who are organizing the event.

It can take place at the university after the official opening ceremony so that we also have the professors and university representatives present.

It can also take place after a company visit and workshop at the company's offices. This way, the networking will be exclusive to the company. Regardless of the location and logistics, there will be a coffee break to enhance the activity, during which we can, at the company's request, have promotional material available, using it to interact with the participants.

The logistics of this activity can also be discussed in more detail in a meeting to adapt it to the company's needs and facilities.

VISIT TO THE COMPANY

As an active part of the academic training of the course, we offer the company the opportunity to host a visit to its offices for the participants and the local IST organizers. With this, we aim to bring the company's activities closer to the students of this course, providing a more detailed insight into its working environment and developing different activities with the participants.

MEET THE STUDENTS
